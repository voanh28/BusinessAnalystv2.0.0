09. Alteryx & Tableau

Alteryx and Tableau

Alteryx and Tableau have free trials you can use before we send you the keys to the professional licenses. You will be receiving your licenses for Alteryx and Tableau after your 7 day free trial of Udacity but before the 14 day trial of Alteryx has ended. The Alteryx trial version is fully functional. Tableau's free trial is indefinite. The main difference between the free trial and the licensed version of Tableau is that it allows you to save locally.

Licenses are sent via email typically on Tuesdays or Thursdays, 7-14 days after your enrollment date.

You will need a Windows OS to install Alteryx on to. If you are using a Mac, you will need to install Parallels or partition your hard drive.

Video on how to partition your hard drive: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8Xc29uP7AFw

There are lessons later in this program that will walk you through downloading the proper versions of the software for your computer. You will not need to use these software tools until you arrive at their respective download lessons. When you do download Alteryx make sure your download 10.6 or there will be UI elements that will not match the videos in this course. 11.0 has the same functionality but some of the configuration for tools will look different.

If you have any specific questions about these licenses please email bizanalyst-support@udacity.com

If you have questions about your Alteryx workflow please direct those questions to Slack or the Forums. The project reviewers are not required or expected to open up your workflows if you submit them in your project. We like to handle workflow issues in Slack or the Forum so other students can learn from the discussion we can have about where you are stuck.
