03. Connecting with Your Community

Connecting with Your Community

Your Nanodegree community will play a huge role in supporting you when you get stuck and in helping you deepen your learning. Getting to know your fellow students will also make your experience a lot more fun!

Forums

To ask questions and get answers for Mentors within 24 hours, head to our forum. You can get there by clicking the forums icon on the left of your classroom or click the forums link in the Resources tab in the upper right. In the forum, the Udacity mentorship team, as well as your fellow students, will be there to help out. You can search to see if someone has already asked a question related to yours, or you can make a new post if no one has. Chances are, someone else is wondering about the same thing you are, so donâ€™t be shy!

If you have any technical issues with Alteryx, post your issues on our forum instead of Alteryx's forum, this helps us get answers to all of our students easily.

Slack

To talk with other students or get quick replies to easy questions, head to the Predictive Analytics for Business Slack. Slack is an online group messaging tool that allows you to connect with all the other enrolled students. We have channels setup for each project as well as a general channel to talk about what is going on in the world of predictive analytics. Click the button below to get access.
